About TruthLens AI

TruthLens AI is an intelligent platform designed to detect fake and manipulated digital content. The system analyzes text, images, audio, and videos using machine learning and deep learning models to help users verify the authenticity of online information.

Our Mission

Our mission is to combat misinformation and improve trust in digital media. TruthLens AI provides powerful AI tools that allow users to quickly analyze and verify content across multiple media formats.

Technologies Used

  • Machine Learning (SVM, CNN)
  • Deep Learning
  • HTML & CSS
  • NumPy, Pandas
  • Scikit-learn
  • OpenCV
  • Librosa (for audio processing)

How It Works

  • Text: TF-IDF with SVM analyzes news content.
  • Image: CNN models detect manipulated images.
  • Audio: MFCC features with ML classifier detect deepfake voices.
  • Video: CNN analyzes video frames to detect deepfake videos.
